Meet Churpi — the ancient hard cheese of the Himalayas, handcrafted right here in the highlands of Arunachal Pradesh by indigenous yak herding families who have perfected this art over generations.

Our yaks roam free at altitudes above 10,000 feet, grazing on wild alpine grass. Their milk is richer, creamier, and more nourishing than anything found in the plains — and that's exactly what goes into every block of our Churpi. Nothing else. No preservatives. No additives. Just milk, tradition, and mountain air. 🌿

Slow-dried and smoke-cured the old way, each piece is packed with protein, calcium, and natural goodness — a warrior's snack for centuries, now making its way to your table.

"When you buy our Churpi, you're not just buying a snack — you're supporting a family in the mountains of Arunachal who has kept this tradition alive through generations."

Chew it slowly like a hardened candy. Crumble it into your soup. Brew it into a warm, comforting tea. However you enjoy it, Churpi delivers a taste that is unmistakably Arunachali. 🍵
